Swap Seal Flow: 
1. Player plays a swap card in round n, the hand is calculated normally 
2. In round n + 1, the player who played the swap card is dealt their hands, except for one less card leaving them with 6 in total 
3. Player is then presented with x amount of cards from the top of the deck face up from the player to select from 
4. Selected card is held in players hand, the other cards are sent to the bottom of the dec k
5. If round n is the finaly round, swap cards are converted into static seals for bonus points
